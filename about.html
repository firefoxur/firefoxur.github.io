Firefox User Research

Hello from Firefox's User Research team! We are small group of design and user experience researchers working alongside the Firefox UX and Product teams to understand and serve our users around the world. We user a wide range of qualitative and quantitative research methods, including: card sorting, diary studies, surveys, in-depth interviews, multivariate testing.

While we currenty only have two qualitative researchers working on Firefox, we hope to grow our team next year. Keep an eye on our open Mozilla Jobs!

Bill Selman

Bill Selman is a Lead User Researcher based in Portland, OR. He is primarily focused on desktop research.

Gemma Petrie

Gemma Petrie is a Senior User Researcher based in Chicago, IL. She is primarily focused on mobile research.
